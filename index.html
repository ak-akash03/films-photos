About Our Editing Lab

Welcome to Ak Creation : Films & Photos

At Ak Creation : Films & Photos, we believe in the power of photography to preserve memories, tell stories, and evoke emotions. With a passion for art and an eye for detail, we specialize in creating stunning visual narratives that resonate with our clients. Whether it's capturing the joy of a wedding, the essence of a product, or the beauty of everyday moments, our goal is to deliver images that are timeless and meaningful.

Who We Are

Ak Creation : Films & Photos was founded by Akash Rathod, a visionary photographer with a deep love for storytelling through the lens. Over the years, Akash has honed his skills, blending technical expertise with creative artistry to deliver breathtaking photographs. Our team is a collective of talented professionals dedicated to exceeding client expectations and turning moments into masterpieces.

Our Studio

Located in the heart of Chalisgaon, our state-of-the-art photography studio is designed to inspire creativity and provide a comfortable environment for our clients. Equipped with advanced lighting systems, backdrops, and the latest photography equipment, our studio is ideal for a variety of shoots, including portraits, product photography, and creative projects.

Features of Our Studio:

- Professional-grade lighting and props.
- A cozy lounge area for clients to relax during sessions.
- High-speed editing workstations for on-the-spot previews.
